Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At U-Money India, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information on our zero-investment task platform.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect basic information necessary for task completion and UPI settlements, such as your registered mobile number and UPI ID. We never ask for sensitive bank passwords or OTPs.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely for processing your earnings and ensuring secure transactions within the U-Money ecosystem. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Zero-Investment Security

U-Money is committed to a safe environment. We will never request any form of deposit, registration fee, or investment from our users. Any request for money on behalf of U-Money is a scam and should be reported immediately.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, please contact our official support channel via the main platform.
